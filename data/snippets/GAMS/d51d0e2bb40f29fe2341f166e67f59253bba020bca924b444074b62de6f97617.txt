Sets
    i     Set of industries
    j     Set of products
    t     Set of time periods

Alias (i, ir);
Alias (j, jr);

Parameters
    cost(i,j) Cost per unit of product j in industry i
    demand(j) Demand for product j
    supply(j) Supply of product j
    revenue(j) Revenue generated by product j
    profit(i) Industry profit in each period;

Variables
    x(i,j) Amount of product j produced in industry i
    y(j)   Binary variable indicating if product j is produced;

Variables
    z         Objective function variable
    cobj      Complements the objective function variable
    c(i,j)    Dual solution for cost constraints i

Positive Variables x, y, z ;

Equations
    BakeryProfit(i)   Compute the objective function variable
    SupplyJa(j)       Demand must be satisfied or greater than 0
    IndustryProfit(i) Compute the profit per industry;
   
BakeryProfit(i)..  profit(i) =l= sum(j, revenue(j)*x(i,j) - cost(i,j)*x(i,j));

SupplyJa(j).. SatisfyDemand(j) =e= supply(j) - demand(j);

IndustryProfit(i).. TotalIndustryProfit =g= sum(j, profit(i)*x(i,j));

Model bakery /all/;
