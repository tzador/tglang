#### Cold-Brew Coffee

`* 1 cup coarsely ground coffee`
`* 4 cups cold water`
`* 1 large jar`

1. Add the coffee grounds to the jar.
2. Slowly pour the cold water over the grounds.
3. Stir the mixture gently.
4. Cover the jar and let it sit in the fridge for at least 12 hours.
5. Strain the mixture using a fine-mesh strainer or cheesecloth.
6. Serve the cold-brew coffee over ice.

*Note: This recipe can easily be doubled or tripled to make larger batches.*
