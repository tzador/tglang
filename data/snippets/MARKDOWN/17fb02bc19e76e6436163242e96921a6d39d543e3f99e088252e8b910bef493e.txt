# List of Animals
- Dog: a domesticated carnivorous mammal with a barking cry and a bushy tail. 
- Cat: a small domesticated mammal with soft fur, retractable claws, and a short pointed snout.
- Parrot: a bird with vibrant feathers, capable of mimicking human speech.
- Horse: a domesticated hoofed mammal with a long mane and tail, used for riding and transportation.
