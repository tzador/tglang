# MARKDOWN Example

1. **Bold text** - `**Bold text**` or `__Bold text__`

2. *Italic text* - `*Italic text*` or `_Italic text_`

3. ***Bold and Italic text*** - `***Bold and Italic text***` or `___Bold and Italic text___`

4. ~~Strikethrough text~~ - `~~Strikethrough text~~`

5. `Inline code` - `` `Inline code` `` or `` `` `Inline code` `` ``
