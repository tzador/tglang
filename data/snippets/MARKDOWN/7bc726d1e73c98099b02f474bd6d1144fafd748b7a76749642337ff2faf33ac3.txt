### Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

*Ingredients:*
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 slices of cheese
- butter

*Instructions:*
1. Heat a pan on medium heat.
2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on the pan.
4. Add one slice of cheese on top of the bread.
5. Place the other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese.
6. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until the bottom bread is golden brown.
7. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the other side is golden brown.
8. Remove from heat and let it cool for a minute before cutting and serving.

*Enjoy your delicious grilled cheese sandwich!*
