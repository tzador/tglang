Text can be written normally in markdown.

## Heading 2

You can also create ordered lists:
1. First item
2. Second item
3. Third item

Or unordered lists:
- Item 1
- Item 2
- Item 3

Links can be added with the [text](url) format:
[Markdown Guide](https://www.markdownguide.org/)

Images can be added with the ![text](image url) format:
![Markdown logo](https://markdown-here.com/img/icon256.png)
