# List of Important Terms

A list can be created using the "- " symbol followed by the item.

- Bread: A staple food made from flour, water, and yeast.
- Butter: A dairy product made from churning milk.
- Milk: An opaque white liquid rich in nutrients.
- Eggs: A reproductive cell typically laid by female animals.
- Flour: A powder made from grinding grains, used for baking.
- Yeast: A type of fungus used in fermentation processes.
- Churning: The process of agitating cream to separate butter.
- Opaque: Not able to be seen through.
- Nutrients: Substances essential for growth and health.
