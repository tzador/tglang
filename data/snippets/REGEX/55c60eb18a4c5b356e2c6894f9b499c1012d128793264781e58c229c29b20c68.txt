/* This regex code snippet is used to check for valid email addresses, with the required format being <local-part>@<domain>.<tld>, where the local-part can contain alphanumeric characters and certain special characters, and the domain and tld can contain alphanumeric characters and periods. */
^[A-Za-z0-9+_.-]+@[A-Za-z0-9.-]+\.[A-Za-z]{2,}$
