// This regex is used to validate email addresses
// It ensures that the email has a valid format with characters before and after the '@' symbol and a valid domain at the end
// It also allows for the use of periods, underscores, and hyphens in the email address

^[a-zA-Z0-9]+[._-]?[a-zA-Z0-9]*@[a-zA-Z0-9]+(.[a-zA-Z0-9]+)*.[a-zA-Z]{2,6}$
