// This regular expression checks if a string contains at least one lowercase letter followed by at least one capital letter, then at least one lowercase letter, followed by at least one digit, and at least one special character (either !, @, #, $, or %). It also requires that the string includes at least 3 words, with the first letter of each word being capitalized.
