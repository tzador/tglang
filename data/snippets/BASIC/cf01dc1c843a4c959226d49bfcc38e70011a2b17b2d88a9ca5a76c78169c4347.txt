'This program calculates the sum of the first 100 positive integers
sum = 0 'initialize sum variable to 0
FOR i = 1 TO 100 'for loop to iterate through first 100 positive integers
    sum = sum + i 'add current integer to sum
NEXT i 'go to next integer
PRINT "The sum of the first 100 positive integers is "; sum 'print sum
END 'end program
