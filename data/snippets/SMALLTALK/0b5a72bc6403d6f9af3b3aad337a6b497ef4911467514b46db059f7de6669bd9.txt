| animals newAnimal |  "Defines two variables, animals and newAnimal"
animals := #(cat dog bird).    "Assigns an array of animal names to the variable animals"
newAnimal := 'bear'.    "Assigns the name 'bear' to the variable newAnimal"
animals add: newAnimal.    "Adds the new animal to the array"
animals do: [:animal |    "Iterates through each element in the array"
    Transcript show: animal, ' makes a sound.'.    "Prints the animal's name followed by 'makes a sound.'"
    Transcript cr.    "Outputs a new line"
]    "Ends the iteration"
