\section{Introduction} % Introduction section
Here, I will introduce my topic and provide background information on it.

\subsection{Research Question} % Subsection for research question
My research question is: What is the impact of social media on interpersonal communication?

\subsection{Objectives} % Subsection for objectives
The objectives of my research are:
\begin{itemize} % Using itemize for a bulleted list
\item To explore the history of social media and its evolution
\item To analyze the effects of social media on face-to-face communication
\item To determine the role of social media in modern society
\end{itemize}

\section{Literature Review} % Literature review section
In this section, I will discuss the existing literature on the topic and highlight key findings.

\subsection{History of Social Media} % Subsection for history of social media
Social media has been around since the late 1990s with the emergence of platforms like Six Degrees and Friendster.

\subsection{Effects on Interpersonal Communication} % Subsection for effects on interpersonal communication
Numerous studies have shown that the use of social media has led to a decrease in face-to-face interaction and has affected the quality of communication.

\subsection{Role in Modern Society} % Subsection for role in modern society
Social media has become an integral part of modern society, with its influence extending to various aspects of people's lives.

\section{Methodology} % Methodology section
In this section, I will explain my research methodology and the tools I used for data collection and analysis.

\subsection{Qualitative Research} % Subsection for qualitative research
I conducted in-depth interviews with individuals from different age groups to gather insights on their experiences with social media and its impact on their interpersonal communication skills.

\subsection{Quantitative Research} % Subsection for quantitative research
I also distributed surveys to a large sample of participants to gather quantitative data and compare it with the qualitative findings.

\subsection{Data Analysis} % Subsection for data analysis
To analyze the data, I used thematic analysis for the qualitative data and statistical analysis for the quantitative data.

\section{Results} % Results section
In this section, I will present and discuss the findings of my research.

\subsection{Decrease in Face-to-Face Communication} % Subsection for decrease in face-to-face communication
The results showed that the use of social media has led to a decrease in face-to-face communication among individuals.

\subsection{Negative Impact on Communication Skills} % Subsection for negative impact on communication skills
The study also revealed that social media has negatively impacted interpersonal communication skills, particularly in the younger generation.

\subsection{Dominance in Modern Society} % Subsection for dominance in modern society
It was found that social media has become the dominant mode of communication in modern society, surpassing face-to-face interaction.

\section{Conclusion} % Conclusion section
Based on the findings, it can be concluded that social media has a significant impact on interpersonal communication and has altered the way we interact with others.
