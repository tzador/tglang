      <source>Cookie Lover's Cookbook</source>
      <ingredients>
        <ingredient>1 cup butter, softened</ingredient>
        <ingredient>1 cup brown sugar</ingredient>
        <ingredient>1/2 cup white sugar</ingredient>
        <ingredient>2 eggs</ingredient>
        <ingredient>2 teaspoons vanilla extract</ingredient>
        <ingredient>2 cups all-purpose flour</ingredient>
        <ingredient>1 teaspoon baking soda</ingredient>
        <ingredient>1 teaspoon salt</ingredient>
        <ingredient>2 cups semisweet chocolate chips</ingredient>
      </ingredients>
      <instructions>
        <step>Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.</step>
        <step>In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.</step>
        <step>Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.</step>
        <step>In a separate bowl, mix together flour, baking soda, and salt.</step>
        <step>Add flour mixture to butter mixture and stir until well combined.</step>
        <step>Stir in chocolate chips.</step>
        <step>Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.</step>
        <step>Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are lightly golden.</step>
        <step>Cool on wire racks and enjoy!</step>
      </instructions>
