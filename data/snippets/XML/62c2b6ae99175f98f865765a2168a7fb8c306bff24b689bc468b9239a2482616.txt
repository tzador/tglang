		<instructions>
			1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
			2. In a small bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
			3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
			4. Add in egg and vanilla extract, mix well.
			5. Gradually add in the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
			6. Stir in chocolate chips.
			7. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
			8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
			9. Let cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
			10. Enjoy your freshly baked chocolate chip cookies!
		</instructions>
