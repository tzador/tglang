: reverse ( seq1 -- seq2 ) (Reverses the sequence given by copying it in reverse order onto another sequence)
  seq>array  (Converts the sequence into an array)
  dup dup 0 do i 1- n - i (Calculates the index of the current element in the new sequence)
  swap array>seq (Converts the array back into a sequence)
; (End of reverse definition)
