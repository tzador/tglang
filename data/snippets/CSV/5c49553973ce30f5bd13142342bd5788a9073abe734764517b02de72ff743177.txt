Examples of CSV data with different delimiters and quotes:

"First Name","Last Name","Age","City"  // basic example with comma
John,Smith,32,"New York"
"Jane","Doe",45,"San Francisco" 

First Name;Last Name;Age;City           // example with semicolon
John;Smith;32;"New York"
Jane;"Doe";45;"San Francisco"

First Name:"Age":"City":"Last Name"      // example with colon
"Tina","21","Chicago","Johnson"
Peter;"30";"London";"Jones"
