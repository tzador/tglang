# This code snippet is a sample YAML file that represents a recipe for chicken parmesan.

# Define dictionary for ingredients
ingredients:
  - chicken:
    - 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    - salt and pepper, to taste
    - 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  - sauce:
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1 onion, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
    - 1 teaspoon of dried basil
    - 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
    - salt and pepper, to taste
  - cheese:
    - 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
    - 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
    - 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

# Define steps for recipe
steps:
  - Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  - Flatten chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness using a meat tenderizer.
  - Season both sides of chicken with salt and pepper.
  - Coat chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.
  - In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  - Cook chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  - Remove chicken from skillet and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  - In the same skillet, add onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  - Stir in crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, and salt and pepper.
  - Simmer for 10 minutes.
  - Pour sauce over chicken in baking dish.
  - Sprinkle parmesan and mozzarella cheese over chicken and sauce.
  - Bake for 20 minutes, until chicken is fully cooked and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  - Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
