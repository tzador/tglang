IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. CODE-SNIPPET.

       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01 INPUT-NUMBER PIC 9(3).
       01 RESULT-NUMBER PIC 9(4).

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
           START-OF-EXECUTION.
           DISPLAY "Please enter a number: ".
           ACCEPT INPUT-NUMBER.
           MULTIPLY 10 BY INPUT-NUMBER GIVING RESULT-NUMBER.
           DISPLAY "The result is: " RESULT-NUMBER.

           COMPUTE RESULT-NUMBER = RESULT-NUMBER + 5.

           PERFORM VARYING INPUT-NUMBER FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL INPUT-NUMBER > 10
               DISPLAY "Current number: " INPUT-NUMBER
           END-PERFORM.

           STOP RUN.
