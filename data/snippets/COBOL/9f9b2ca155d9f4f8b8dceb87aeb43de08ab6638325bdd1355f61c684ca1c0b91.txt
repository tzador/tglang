IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. CALCULATE-EMPLOYEE-PAY.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 EMPLOYEE-RECORD.
   05 EMPLOYEE-ID PIC X(6).
   05 EMPLOYEE-NAME PIC X(20).
   05 EMPLOYEE-DEPARTMENT PIC X(10).
   05 EMPLOYEE-SALARY PIC 9(7)V99.
01 TAX-RATES.
   05 FEDERAL-RATE PIC 9(3)V99.
   05 STATE-RATE PIC 9(3)V99.
   05 LOCAL-RATE PIC 9(3)V99.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC.
   PERFORM INITIALIZATION.
   PERFORM INPUT-EMPLOYEE-DATA.
   PERFORM CALCULATE-SALARY.
   PERFORM DISPLAY-RESULTS.
   PERFORM EXIT-PROGRAM.

INITIALIZATION.
   MOVE 0 TO FEDERAL-RATE, STATE-RATE, LOCAL-RATE.

INPUT-EMPLOYEE-DATA.
   DISPLAY "ENTER EMPLOYEE ID:" WITH CONVERSION.
   ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-ID.
   DISPLAY "ENTER EMPLOYEE NAME:" WITH CONVERSION.
   ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-NAME.
   DISPLAY "ENTER EMPLOYEE DEPARTMENT:" WITH CONVERSION.
   ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-DEPARTMENT.
   DISPLAY "ENTER EMPLOYEE SALARY:" WITH CONVERSION.
   ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-SALARY.

CALCULATE-SALARY.
   MULTIPLY EMPLOYEE-SALARY BY FEDERAL-RATE GIVING FED-TAX.
   MULTIPLY EMPLOYEE-SALARY BY STATE-RATE GIVING STATE-TAX.
   MULTIPLY EMPLOYEE-SALARY BY LOCAL-RATE GIVING LOCAL-TAX.
   ADD FED-TAX, STATE-TAX, LOCAL-TAX TO EMPLOYEE-SALARY.

DISPLAY-RESULTS.
   DISPLAY "EMPLOYEE ID:" EMPLOYEE-ID.
   DISPLAY "EMPLOYEE NAME:" EMPLOYEE-NAME.
   DISPLAY "EMPLOYEE DEPARTMENT:" EMPLOYEE-DEPARTMENT.
   DISPLAY "EMPLOYEE SALARY:" EMPLOYEE-SALARY.
   DISPLAY "FEDERAL TAX:" FED-TAX.
   DISPLAY "STATE TAX:" STATE-TAX.
   DISPLAY "LOCAL TAX:" LOCAL-TAX.
   DISPLAY "NET SALARY:" NET-SALARY.

EXIT-PROGRAM.
   STOP RUN.
