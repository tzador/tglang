*> Program to calculate the sum of an array
IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. SUM-ARRAY.

DATA DIVISION.
* Defining variables
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 COUNT PIC 9(2).
01 SUM PIC S999.
01 ARRAY OCCURS 10 TIMES PIC 9(3).

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC.
* Initializing variables
PERFORM VARYING COUNT FROM 1 BY 1
    UNTIL COUNT > 10
    MOVE 0 TO ARRAY(COUNT)
END-PERFORM.

* Assigning values to the array
MOVE 10 TO ARRAY(1).
MOVE 20 TO ARRAY(2).
MOVE 30 TO ARRAY(3).
MOVE 40 TO ARRAY(4).
MOVE 50 TO ARRAY(5).
MOVE 60 TO ARRAY(6).
MOVE 70 TO ARRAY(7).
MOVE 80 TO ARRAY(8).
MOVE 90 TO ARRAY(9).
MOVE 100 TO ARRAY(10).

* Adding all elements in the array
PERFORM VARYING COUNT FROM 1 BY 1
    UNTIL COUNT > 10
    ADD ARRAY(COUNT) TO SUM
END-PERFORM.

* Displaying the sum
DISPLAY "The sum of the array is: " SUM.

STOP RUN.
