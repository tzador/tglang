* This is a COBOL program that calculates the average of ten numbers
IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. AVERAGE.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 NUMBERS OCCURS 10 TIMES.
   05 NUM-INPUT PIC 9(3).

01 SUM PIC 9(6)V99 VALUE 0.
01 AVERAGE PIC 9(4)V99.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC.
   PERFORM READ-INPUT-LOOP THRU READ-INPUT-EXIT.
   COMPUTE AVERAGE = SUM / 10.
   DISPLAY "The average is " AVERAGE.

READ-INPUT-LOOP.
   DISPLAY "Enter a number:".
   ACCEPT NUM-INPUT.
   COMPUTE SUM = SUM + NUM-INPUT.
   MULTIPLY NUM-INPUT BY 0.1 GIVING NUM-INPUT.
READ-INPUT-EXIT.
   EXIT.

STOP RUN.
