IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. SAMPLE.
AUTHOR. JOHN SMITH.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 EMPLOYEE-TABLE OCCURS 100 TIMES.
    05 EMPLOYEE-NAME PIC X(20).
    05 EMPLOYEE-ID PIC 9(5).
    05 EMPLOYEE-SALARY PIC 9(6)V99.

01 TOTAL-SALARY PIC 9(8)V99.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-PROCEDURE.
    PERFORM INIT-TABLE.
    PERFORM CALCULATE-SALARY.
    DISPLAY "Total Salary: " TOTAL-SALARY.
    STOP RUN.

INIT-TABLE.
    INITIALIZE EMPLOYEE-TABLE.
    MOVE "John Smith" TO EMPLOYEE-NAME(1).
    MOVE 12345 TO EMPLOYEE-ID(1).
    MOVE 5000.50 TO EMPLOYEE-SALARY(1).
    MOVE "Jane Smith" TO EMPLOYEE-NAME(2).
    MOVE 67890 TO EMPLOYEE-ID(2).
    MOVE 7500.25 TO EMPLOYEE-SALARY(2).

CALCULATE-SALARY.
    PERFORM VARYING I FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL I > 100
        IF EMPLOYEE-ID(I) = ZERO
            CONTINUE
        ELSE
            COMPUTE TOTAL-SALARY = TOTAL-SALARY + EMPLOYEE-SALARY(I)
    END-PERFORM.
