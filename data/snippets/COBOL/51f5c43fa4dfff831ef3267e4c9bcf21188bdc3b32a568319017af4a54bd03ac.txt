* This is a simple COBOL program that calculates the sum of two numbers

IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. SUM.
DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 NUM1 PIC 9(3).
01 NUM2 PIC 9(3).
01 SUM PIC 9(4).
PROCEDURE DIVISION.
ACCEPT NUM1.
ACCEPT NUM2.
COMPUTE SUM = NUM1 + NUM2.
DISPLAY "The sum of " NUM1 " and " NUM2 " is " SUM.
STOP RUN.
