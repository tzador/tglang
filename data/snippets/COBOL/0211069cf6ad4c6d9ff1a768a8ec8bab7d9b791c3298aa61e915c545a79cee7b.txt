IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. SAMPLE-PROGRAM.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 EMPLOYEE-RECORD.
   05 EMPLOYEE-NAME        PIC X(30).
   05 EMPLOYEE-ID          PIC 9(5).
   05 EMPLOYEE-DEPARTMENT  PIC X(20).
   05 EMPLOYEE-SALARY      PIC 9(6)V99.

01 RAISE-RATE              PIC 9(3)V99 VALUE 0.05.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-PROCESS.
    PERFORM GET-EMPLOYEE-DATA
    PERFORM CALCULATE-NEW-SALARY
    PERFORM DISPLAY-NEW-SALARY
    STOP RUN.

GET-EMPLOYEE-DATA.
    DISPLAY "Enter employee name: "
    ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-NAME.
    DISPLAY "Enter employee ID: "
    ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-ID.
    DISPLAY "Enter employee department: "
    ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-DEPARTMENT.
    DISPLAY "Enter employee salary: "
    ACCEPT EMPLOYEE-SALARY.

CALCULATE-NEW-SALARY.
    COMPUTE NEW-SALARY = EMPLOYEE-SALARY + (EMPLOYEE-SALARY * RAISE-RATE).

DISPLAY-NEW-SALARY.
    DISPLAY "Employee Name: " EMPLOYEE-NAME
    DISPLAY "Employee ID: " EMPLOYEE-ID
    DISPLAY "Employee Department: " EMPLOYEE-DEPARTMENT
    DISPLAY "New Salary: " NEW-SALARY.
