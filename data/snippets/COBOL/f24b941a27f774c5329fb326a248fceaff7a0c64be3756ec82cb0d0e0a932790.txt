IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. CALCULATE-TOTAL.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 ITEM-CODE PIC X(10) VALUE 'A1B2C3D4E5'.
01 QUANTITY PIC 9(3) VALUE 25.
01 PRICE PIC 9(5)V99 VALUE 12345.
01 TAX-RATE PIC 9(3)V99 VALUE 8.5.
01 TOTAL-PRICE PIC 9(6)V99.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MULTIPLY QUANTITY BY PRICE GIVING TOTAL-PRICE.
COMPUTE TOTAL-PRICE = TOTAL-PRICE + (TOTAL-PRICE * TAX-RATE / 100).
DISPLAY 'The total price for item ' ITEM-CODE ' is $' TOTAL-PRICE.
