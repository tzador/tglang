IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. EXAMPLE.
AUTHOR. JOHN DOE.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 CUSTOMER-RECORD.
05 CUST-NO PIC X(6).
05 CUST-NAME PIC X(20).
05 CUST-ADDRESS.
10 CUST-STREET PIC X(30).
10 CUST-CITY PIC X(20).
10 CUST-STATE PIC X(2).
10 CUST-ZIP PIC X(5).

01 ORDER-RECORD.
05 ORDER-NO PIC 9(5).
05 CUST-NO PIC X(6).
05 ORDER-DATE PIC 9(8).
05 NUM-ITEMS PIC 99.

01 ITEM-RECORD.
05 ITEM-NO PIC 9(4).
05 ITEM-DESC PIC X(30).
05 ITEM-QTY PIC 9(2).
05 ITEM-PRICE PIC 9(5)V99.

01 ORDER-ITEMS OCCURS 10 TIMES.
05 ITEM-NO PIC 9(4).
05 ITEM-QTY PIC 9(2).

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC SECTION.

ACCEPT CUSTOMER-RECORD FROM CONSOLE.
PERFORM CALCULATE-ORDER.
PERFORM PRINT-INVOICE.

STOP RUN.

CALCULATE-ORDER.
MULTIPLY NUM-ITEMS BY 0.1 GIVING DISCOUNT.
COMPUTE TOTAL-AMOUNT = NUM-ITEMS * ITEM-PRICE.

PRINT-INVOICE.
DISPLAY "CUSTOMER NO: " CUST-NO.
DISPLAY "CUSTOMER NAME: " CUST-NAME.
DISPLAY "CUSTOMER ADDRESS: " CUST-ADDRESS.
DISPLAY "ORDER NO: " ORDER-NO.
DISPLAY "ORDER DATE: " ORDER-DATE.
DISPLAY "DISCOUNT: " DISCOUNT.
DISPLAY "TOTAL AMOUNT: " TOTAL-AMOUNT.
DISPLAY "ORDERED ITEMS: ".
PERFORM VARYING I FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL I > NUM-ITEMS
    DISPLAY "ITEM NO: " ORDER-ITEMS(I).
END-PERFORM.
