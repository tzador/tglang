IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. EXAMPLE.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 STUDENTS.
   05 STUDENT1.
      10 STUDENT-ID PIC 9(5).
      10 STUDENT-NAME PIC X(20).
      10 STUDENT-GRADE PIC 9(2).
   05 STUDENT2.
      10 STUDENT-ID PIC 9(5).
      10 STUDENT-NAME PIC X(20).
      10 STUDENT-GRADE PIC 9(2).

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC.
   MOVE 12345 TO STUDENT1-STUDENT-ID.
   MOVE 'John Doe' TO STUDENT1-STUDENT-NAME.
   MOVE 85 TO STUDENT1-STUDENT-GRADE.
   MOVE 54321 TO STUDENT2-STUDENT-ID.
   MOVE 'Jane Smith' TO STUDENT2-STUDENT-NAME.
   MOVE 92 TO STUDENT2-STUDENT-GRADE.
   DISPLAY 'Student 1 name: ' STUDENT1-STUDENT-NAME.
   DISPLAY 'Student 2 ID: ' STUDENT2-STUDENT-ID.
   IF STUDENT1-STUDENT-GRADE > STUDENT2-STUDENT-GRADE
      DISPLAY 'Student 1 has a higher grade.'
   ELSE
      DISPLAY 'Student 2 has a higher grade.'
   END-IF.
   
STOP RUN.
