IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. EXAMPLE.
AUTHOR. COBOL GEEK.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01  NUM-ARRAY     PIC S9(4) OCCURS 50 TIMES.
01  INDEX         PIC S9(4).
01  RESULT        PIC S9(9)V99.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC.
    PERFORM VARYING INDEX FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL INDEX > 50
        COMPUTE RESULT = (NUM-ARRAY(INDEX) * 250) - 100
        DISPLAY "Result for index " INDEX ":" RESULT
    END-PERFORM.
EXIT PROGRAM.
