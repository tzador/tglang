IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. MY-PROGRAM.

       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.

       01 EMPLOYEE-TABLE.
          05 EMPLOYEE-RECORD OCCURS 10 TIMES.
             10 EMPLOYEE-ID PIC X(10).
             10 EMPLOYEE-NAME PIC X(20).
             10 EMPLOYEE-AGE PIC 99.
             10 EMPLOYEE-SALARY PIC 9(6)V99.

       LINKAGE SECTION.
       01 INPUT-PARAMETERS.
          05 IN-EMPLOYEE-ID PIC X(10).
          05 IN-EMPLOYEE-NAME PIC X(20).

       PROCEDURE DIVISION USING INPUT-PARAMETERS.

       START.
          PERFORM INITIALIZE-TABLE.
          PERFORM READ-EMPLOYEE UNTIL EMPLOYEE-RECORD = EMPLOYEE-TABLE(10).
          PERFORM DISPLAY-RESULTS.
          STOP RUN.

       INITIALIZE-TABLE.
          SET EMPLOYEE-RECORD TO EMPLOYEE-TABLE(1)

       READ-EMPLOYEE.
          READ EMPLOYEE-FILE
             AT END MOVE 'EOF' TO EMPLOYEE-RECORD.

       DISPLAY-RESULTS.
          DISPLAY "Employee ID: " IN-EMPLOYEE-ID.
          DISPLAY "Employee Name: " IN-EMPLOYEE-NAME.
          DISPLAY "Average Salary: " (TOTAL-EMPLOYEE-SALARY/EMPLOYEE-INDEX).
          DISPLAY "Highest Salary: " MAXIMUM-EMPLOYEE-SALARY.
          DISPLAY "Lowest Salary: " MINIMUM-EMPLOYEE-SALARY.

       END-PROGRAM.
          EXIT.
