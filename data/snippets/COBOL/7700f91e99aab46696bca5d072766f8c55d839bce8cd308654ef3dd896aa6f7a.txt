IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. EXAMPLE.
* This program calculates the average of a set of numbers
DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 NUMBERS.
    05 NUM OCCURS 10 TIMES PIC 9(3).
01 TOTAL PIC 9(4).
01 AVG PIC 9(4).
PROCEDURE DIVISION.
MAIN-LOGIC.
    PERFORM INITIALIZE.
    PERFORM GET-NUMBERS.
    PERFORM CALCULATE-AVG.
    PERFORM DISPLAY-AVG.
EXIT-PROGRAM.
    STOP RUN.
INITIALIZE.
    MOVE ZEROS TO TOTAL, AVG.
GET-NUMBERS.
    DISPLAY "Enter 10 numbers:".
    PERFORM VARYING I FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL I > 10
        ACCEPT NUMBERS(I).
    END-PERFORM.
CALCULATE-AVG.
    PERFORM VARYING I FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL I > 10
        ADD NUMBERS(I) TO TOTAL.
    END-PERFORM.
    COMPUTE AVG = TOTAL / 10.
DISPLAY-AVG.
    DISPLAY "The average is " AVG.
