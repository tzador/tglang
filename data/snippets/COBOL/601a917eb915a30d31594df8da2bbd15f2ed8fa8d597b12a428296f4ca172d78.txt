IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. SAMPLE-PROGRAM.
AUTHOR. FIRSTNAME LASTNAME.
* This is a sample COBOL program that performs various calculations
DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
* Declare variables
01 NUM1 PIC 9(3) VALUE 100.
01 NUM2 PIC 9(3) VALUE 50.
01 RESULT PIC 9(3).
01 TEMP PIC 9(4).
01 TEMP2 PIC 9(4).
PROCEDURE DIVISION.
* Add NUM1 and NUM2
ADD NUM1 TO NUM2 GIVING RESULT.
DISPLAY "The result of addition is: " RESULT.
* Subtract NUM1 from NUM2
SUBTRACT NUM1 FROM NUM2 GIVING RESULT.
DISPLAY "The result of subtraction is: " RESULT.
* Multiply NUM1 and NUM2
MULTIPLY NUM1 BY NUM2 GIVING TEMP.
MOVE TEMP TO RESULT.
DISPLAY "The result of multiplication is: " RESULT.
* Divide NUM1 by NUM2
DIVIDE NUM1 BY NUM2 GIVING TEMP2.
MOVE TEMP2 TO RESULT.
DISPLAY "The result of division is: " RESULT.
STOP RUN.
