'This code snippet will demonstrate a for loop that iterates through and displays numbers 1 to 10 in the console

'Create a variable to store the starting number of the loop
Dim startNumber
startNumber = 1

'Create a for loop that will iterate from 1 to 10
For i = startNumber To 10
    'Display the current iteration number in the console
    MsgBox i
Next

'End of code snippet.
