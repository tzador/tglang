' This is a VBScript program that calculates the multiplication of two numbers and prints the result

Option Explicit ' Forces variable declaration

Dim num1, num2, result ' Declaring variables

num1 = 5
num2 = 10

result = num1 * num2 ' Performs multiplication and assigns result to variable

WScript.Echo "The result of " & num1 & " x " & num2 & " is " & result ' Prints the result

' Output: The result of 5 x 10 is 50
