h2. Text Formatting Options

*bold text* - Surround the text with asterisks to make it bold.
_italicized text_ - Surround the text with underscores to make it italicized.
+hightlighted text+ - Surround the text with plus signs to highlight it.
-deleted text- - Surround the text with hyphens to show it as deleted.
