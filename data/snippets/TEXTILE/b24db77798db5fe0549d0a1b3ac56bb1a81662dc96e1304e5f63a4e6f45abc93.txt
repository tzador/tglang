p(o). This is a paragraph with some _emphasized_ and __strong text__.

This is a p tag in textile that starts with "(o)", indicating it is an "outer" paragraph.
Within the p tag, we have plain text and some emphasized and strong text using textile's markdown syntax.
Emphasized text is indicated with a single underscore, while strong text is indicated with double underscores.
