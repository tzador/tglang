* h1. Overview
    Textile is a lightweight markup language that allows for easy formatting of text in plain text environment.
    With Textile, you can add headings, lists, links, images, and other formatting elements using a simple and intuitive syntax.
    Textile is commonly used in web development and content management systems, making it a great choice for creating dynamic and visually appealing content.
